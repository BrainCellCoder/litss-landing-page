About LITSS

Simplifying healthcare operations through powerful software.

LITSS (Lab & Clinic Management System) is a next-generation platform built to streamline and automate core operations of diagnostic labs and clinics. From patient management and doctor scheduling to billing and reporting, LITSS empowers healthcare professionals with tools that are easy to use and tailored for performance and compliance.

  • Simplified reporting workflows
  • Accurate billing & invoicing
  • Mobile & web-based accessibility
  • Scalable from small clinics to large labs
